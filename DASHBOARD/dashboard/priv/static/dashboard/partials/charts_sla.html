HCOPSCC-1

Represents the total number of calls received at the ASC-HCM Contact Center that are answered by an agent in 40 seconds or less, divided by the total number of calls received in the ASC-HCM Contact Center.

HCOPSCC-2

To show the total percentage of cases with resolutions within Level 1 and Level 2 applied with a time-stamp of 4 business days or less divided by the total number of cases created by HCM Contact Center Level 1 or Level 2 agents.
